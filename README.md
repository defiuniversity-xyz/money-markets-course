## üí° Key Principles

1. **Safety First**: Understanding risk parameters protects your capital. Health Factor is your most important metric.

2. **Architecture Matters**: Monolithic protocols (Aave) offer safety and simplicity. Modular protocols (Morpho, Euler) offer efficiency and customization but require more due diligence.

3. **Start Simple**: Begin with stablecoin lending before using crypto as collateral. Master basics before advanced strategies.

4. **Gas Economics**: Layer 2 solutions make active management viable. Choose networks wisely.

5. **Risk Isolation**: Understand whether your protocol pools risk or isolates it. This determines your exposure.
